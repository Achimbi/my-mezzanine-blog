Who we are 

We are a Christian blogging platform which focusses on revealing Jesus fulfilled in the Law, the Psalms, the Prophets, and the whole of Scripture. We believe that salvation is found in Christ alone (Solus Christus). Basically, we are Christocentric theologians who seek to reveal Jesus through blogging.

What we do 

We regularly create and share articles which are teachings about our Lord Jesus Christ, and we seek to reach those in and out of the Body of Christ with the message of our Lord Jesus Christ, through social media. We also seek to create an online community of those who seek to see Jesus revealed in the Scriptures.

Where we are 

At the moment we are currently located in West Africa. And we are specifically found in Cameroon. However, our physical location is not the extent of our reach. For thanks to social media and cyberspace, we intend to make our impact as global as possible. Thanks to the internet, our range of impact is global.

Why we do what we do 

We do what we do because we must testify of our Lord Jesus Christ, and we must avail ourselves of every opportunity to make our Lord Jesus Christ known. For all the nations of the earth must hear of Him before He returns. And we strongly believe the Church must return to focussing on Jesus. Come Lord Jesus !

Our Key Verse:


For in Christ lives all the fullness of God in a human body.
Colossians 2:9 AMP
